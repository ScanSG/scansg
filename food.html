🍜 Singapore Food & Lifestyle Guide 2025

Complete list of hawker centres, malls, must-try food & nature spots — by region. No blur-blur.

🏙️ Central Core

🍽️ Hawkers

  • Lau Pa Sat: Historic market at 18 Raffles Quay, famous for satay and seafood.
  • Chinatown Food Street: Open-air hawker centre with diverse local dishes.
  • Amoy Street Food Centre (Blk 44): Popular for zi char and local delights.
  • Maxwell Food Centre: Famous for Tian Tian Chicken Rice.
  • Raffles Place Food Scene: Lunch hub for office workers.

🛍️ Malls

  • Raffles City: Connected to City Hall MRT, rooftop garden.
  • Plaza Singapura: Near Dhoby Ghaut, known for dining & entertainment.
  • ION Orchard: Luxury shopping on Orchard Road.
  • Marina Bay Sands Shoppes: High-end brands and dining.

🔥 Must-Try

  • Tian Tian Chicken Rice at Maxwell
  • Satay at Lau Pa Sat
  • Hainanese Chicken Rice at Chinatown Complex

🌳 Parks & Nature

  • Gardens by the Bay: Supertree Grove, Flower Dome
  • Marina Bay Promenade: Skyline views
  • Helix Bridge: DNA-inspired walkway
🌆 Central South

🍽️ Hawkers

  • Tanjong Pagar Food Scene: Diverse options, from hawker centres to upscale.
  • Outram Park Food Scene: Mix of hawker centres and restaurants.
  • Redhill Food Scene: Local eateries near Redhill MRT.
  • Queenstown Food Scene: Strong local food culture.
  • VivoCity Food Court: Largest mall’s food options.

🛍️ Malls

  • VivoCity: Largest mall, connected to Sentosa.
  • Alexandra Retail Centre: Lifestyle mall near Queenstown.
  • Queensway Shopping Centre: Local mall in Queenstown.

🔥 Must-Try

  • Char Kway Teow at Outram Park
  • Satay at Adam Road Food Centre

🌳 Parks & Nature

  • Kent Ridge Park: Scenic trails and views
  • Telok Blangah Hill Park: Waterfalls and greenery
🌅 East

🍽️ Hawkers

  • East Coast Lagoon Food Village: BBQ seafood, drinks by the sea.
  • Katong Laksa Village: Home of Peranakan laksa.
  • Changi Village Food Centre: Local hawker centre near airport.
  • Bedok Food Centre (Blk 85): Large, popular hawker centre.

🛍️ Malls

  • Katong Shopping Belt: Peranakan culture & unique shops.
  • 112 Katong: Modern mall with lifestyle focus.

🔥 Must-Try

  • Katong Laksa
  • BBQ Seafood at ECP

🌳 Parks & Nature

  • East Coast Park: Cycling, picnics, beach
  • Bedok Reservoir Park: Waterfront views
🏭 West

🍽️ Hawkers

  • Chinese Garden Food Centre (Blk 505): Large hawker centre near Chinese Garden MRT.
  • Jurong West Hawker Centre (Blk 500): Serves Jurong West community.
  • Boon Lay Place Food Village: Near Boon Lay MRT.
  • West Coast Food Centre (Blk 301): Serves Clementi/West Coast.

🛍️ Malls

  • Jurong Point: Major mall in Jurong East.
  • Jem: Modern mall with cantilevered design.
  • Westgate: Modern mall with rooftop garden.

🔥 Must-Try

  • Rojak at Joo Choon Market
  • Mee Rebus at Jurong Point

🌳 Parks & Nature

  • Jurong Lake Gardens: Beautiful lakeside park
  • Coney Island Park: Wild, natural island park
  • Admiralty Park: Green space with fitness corners
🏞️ North

🍽️ Hawkers

  • Ang Mo Kio Food Centre (Blk 226A): Well-known hawker centre.
  • Bishan Food Hawker: Famous for chilli crab mee sua.
  • Yishun Park Hawker Centre (Blk 235): Serves Yishun community.
  • Sembawang Hawkers: Food court with seaside views.

🔥 Must-Try

  • Roast Duck Rice at Yishun
  • Chilli Crab Mee Sua at Bishan

🌳 Parks & Nature

  • Sembawang Park: Beach, kampong vibe
  • Punggol Waterway Park: Scenic boardwalks
  • Kranji Countryside: Farms and rustic charm
🏘️ North-East

🍽️ Hawkers

  • Hougang 10 Food Centre (Blk 109): Popular hawker centre.
  • Serangoon Gardens Food Centre (Blk 49A): Local gem.

🔥 Must-Try

  • Laksa at Serangoon Gardens
  • Bak Kut Teh at Hougang
