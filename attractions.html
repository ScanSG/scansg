🌆 Central Singapore

  • Marina Bay Sands SkyPark Paid

    Iconic infinity pool and panoramic city views.

    💡 Hack: Visit the Observation Deck (free) at MBS Shoppes Level B1 for skyline views. Or go to CE LA VI bar (min. spend) for cheaper access than SkyPark.

    📸 Best photo angle: From Helix Bridge at night using 10x zoom.

  • Gardens by the Bay (Cloud Forest & Flower Dome) Paid

    Supertrees, futuristic domes, and floral wonders.

    💡 Hack: Free entry to Supertree Grove at night during light show (7:45 & 8:45 PM). Locals skip paid domes and picnic under Supertrees.

    ⏰ Best time: Arrive 30 mins before light show. Bring a picnic mat and snacks!

  • Singapore Flyer Paid

    165m tall observation wheel with 360° city views.

    💡 Hack: Skip the Flyer. Go to 1-Altitude SkyBar (free entry, min. drink) or CE LA VI for same views at lower cost.

  • Singapore River Cruise Paid

    40-minute boat ride through Clarke Quay, Boat Quay & Marina Bay.

    💡 Hack: Free alternative: Walk the river promenade from Robertson Quay to Marina Bay. Same views, zero cost.

  • Chinatown Rickshaw Tour Paid

    Fun, nostalgic ride through historic streets with commentary.

  • National Gallery Singapore (Indoor) Paid

    World-class Southeast Asian art collection.

  • ArtScience Museum (Exhibition) Paid

    Futuristic exhibitions on art, science, and tech.

  • Marina Bay Sands Canal Cruise Paid

    Charming 6-minute boat ride through the luxurious MBS Shoppes, inspired by Venetian canals.

    💡 Hack: Free for kids under 3. Buy tickets at the booth near Carousel Food Court — avoid weekend peak hours (4–8 PM). Open until 10 PM daily.

    📸 Pro Tip: Best photos from the back seat! Combine with a visit to CE LA VI SkyBar or ArtScience Museum for a full MBS evening.

  • Marina Bay Yacht / Speedboat Ride Paid

    Thrilling 30-min speedboat tour around Marina Bay skyline.

    💡 Hack: Book sunset slot via Klook for golden hour views. Group discounts available.

  • The Battle Box at Fort Canning Paid

    Singapore’s only underground WWII command center — immersive 75-minute guided tour.

    💡 Hack: Book the first or last slot of the day for smaller groups. Free audio guide included. Kids under 5 enter free.

    📸 Pro Tip: Combine with Fort Canning Park visit — same location. Great for history buffs and families.

  • Merlion Park Free

    Home to Singapore’s iconic Merlion statue. Best at sunset.

    💡 Hack: Best fireworks view on NYE — arrive by 8 PM to secure a spot. Or watch from Esplanade Bridge for wider angles.

  • Helix Bridge Free

    Iconic DNA-inspired pedestrian bridge with great photo ops.

  • Fort Canning Park Free

    Historic green space with trails, ruins, and events.

    💡 Hack: Hidden underground tunnels (Peranakan Museum side). Also a great NYE viewing spot away from crowds.

  • Henderson Waves Free

    Singapore’s highest pedestrian bridge with jungle views.

    💡 Hack: Access via Mount Faber Park or Telok Blangah Hill Park. Best at golden hour.

  • Telok Ayer Green Free

    Small park near Chinatown MRT. Great for a quick break.

  • Capitol Singapore Sky Lawn Free

    Free rooftop garden with city views.

  • National Gallery Singapore (Outdoor) Free

    Stunning architecture & light shows. Outdoor courtyards are free.

    💡 Hack: Free entry to DBS Singapore Gallery (Level 1). Permanent local art exhibit.

  • ArtScience Museum (Exterior) Free

    Lotus-inspired building with free fountain shows at night.

  • Esplanade Theatres (Outdoor) Free

    Free performances and events at the "Durian" building.

  • The Arts House Free

    Historic building with free exhibitions and events.

  • OCBC SkyPark @ Marina Bay Free

    Car-free cycling and jogging track along Marina Bay.

    💡 Hack: Rent a bike from Anywheel nearby. Open 7 AM – 7 PM daily.

🏝️ Central South & Sentosa

  • Universal Studios Singapore Paid

    Hollywood-themed rides and attractions.

    💡 Hack: Buy tickets via Klook (save 15%). Visit on weekdays. Free entry for kids under 3.

  • Singapore Oceanarium Paid

    One of the world’s largest aquariums.

    💡 Hack: Book a night sleepover (PADI dive or family camp) — includes entry, food, and exclusive access.

  • Adventure Cove Waterpark Paid

    Tropical-themed water park with slides and attractions.

    💡 Hack: Combo tickets with S.E.A. Aquarium save 20%. Go on weekdays for shorter queues.

  • Wings of Time Show Paid

    Evening show with water, fire, lasers, and storytelling.

    💡 Hack: Watch for free from Siloso Beach — clear view with music. No ticket needed.

  • Singapore Cable Car Paid

    Scenic ride with views of the harbor and Sentosa.

    💡 Hack: Use NETS FlashPay or EZ-Link for slight discount. Or walk the Sentosa Boardwalk for free harbor views.

  • Madame Tussauds Singapore Paid

    Wax museum with lifelike figures of celebrities.

    💡 Hack: Free photo op with the outdoor Marvel figures near the entrance — no ticket needed.

  • SkyHelix Sentosa Paid

    360° panoramic ride in an open-air gondola.

    💡 Hack: Visit at sunset for best photos. Combo tickets with other Sentosa attractions save money.

  • Dolphin Island (Coming Soon) Paid (Opening 2025)

    New interactive marine habitat at Resorts World Sentosa — set to offer up-close dolphin encounters and education zones.

    💡 Hack: Pre-book tickets via Klook or Pelago when announced. Expect early-bird discounts and soft-launch access.

    🔔 Stay Updated: This will replace the old Dolphin Island. Follow RWS Official Social Media for launch dates — we’ll update scansg.com the moment it opens!

  • Mount Faber Luge & Skyride Paid

    Thrilling downhill luge cart ride and scenic sky gondola.

    💡 Hack: Buy unlimited ride pass via Klook. Best for families and couples.

  • Megazip Adventure Park Paid

    Asia’s longest zip line over the beach.

  • Trickeye Museum Paid

    Interactive 3D illusion art gallery — perfect for Instagram.

  • Escape Theme Park Paid

    Adventure park with obstacle courses, rope walks, and slides.

  • Sensoryscape at Gardens by the Bay (Sentosa Extension) Paid

    Immersive sensory trail with light, sound, and fragrance.

    💡 Hack: Open late — great for post-dinner walks. Free for children under 3.

  • Sentosa Boardwalk Free

    Scenic walkway connecting VivoCity to Sentosa Island.

  • Siloso Beach Free

    Vibrant beach with music, games, and water sports.

    💡 Hack: Best free NYE beach party — live music, countdown, and fireworks view. Arrive early!

  • Palawan Beach Free

    Family-friendly beach with the Southernmost Point of Asia.

    💡 Hack: Cross the rope bridge to private island — few tourists know this!

  • Tanjong Beach Free

    Quieter beach, closer to Resorts World Sentosa.

  • Tanjong Beach Club Free (Beach Access)

    Chic beach club with relaxed vibe. Free beach access, pay for food/drinks.

  • Sentosa Nature Discovery Free

    Canopy walk and mangrove boardwalk.

  • Fort Siloso Free

    Historic WWII fort with tunnels and cannons.

  • Imbiah Lookout (Free Areas) Free

    Scenic cliffside views of the sea.

  • Sentosa Cove Boardwalk Free

    Luxury waterfront walk with views of yachts and Marina Bay.

🏖️ East Singapore

  • Canopy Park @ Jewel Changi Paid

    Topiaries, mazes, and bouncing nets.

    💡 Hack: Free access to Rain Vortex and Forest Valley. Canopy Park attractions cost extra — skip if on a budget.

  • Rain Vortex @ Jewel Changi Free

    World’s tallest indoor waterfall. Open 24/7.

    💡 Hack: Light show every 15-30 mins from 7:30 PM to 10 PM. Best views from B2 and B3.

  • East Coast Park Free

    Perfect for cycling, BBQs, and beach picnics.

    💡 Hack: Rent bikes from oBike or Anywheel (under $3/hour). NYE fireworks view from ECP near Marine Cove.

  • Punggol Waterway Park Free

    Scenic boardwalks and family trails. Try pedal boats!

  • Coney Island Park Free

    Rustic island with cycling trails and abandoned ruins.

    💡 Hack: Take bus 36 from Punggol MRT. Best for nature lovers and photographers.

  • Changi Beach Park Free

    Quiet beach with views of cargo ships.

  • Pasir Ris Park Free

    Family-friendly with mangroves and water play areas.

  • Changi Village Food Centre Free

    Local hawker centre near the airport.

  • Changi Point Coastal Walk Free

    Scenic 2.2km coastal trail with mangroves and sea views.

  • Dunman Loop Park Free

    Hidden green space with fitness corners.

  • Siglap Park Free

    Quiet park near East Coast.

🎢 West Singapore

  • Night Safari Paid

    World’s first nocturnal zoo.

    💡 Hack: Buy combo with Zoo & River Safari via Klook. Free entry for under 3s. Go on full moon nights for natural lighting.

  • Singapore Zoo Paid

    Top-rated zoo with open enclosures.

  • River Safari Paid

    Home to giant pandas and river animals.

  • Haw Par Villa Free

    Bizarre, colorful statues of Chinese folklore.

    💡 Hack: Free entry to Ten Courts of Hell — wildest attraction. Visit early to avoid heat.

  • Jurong Lake Gardens Free

    Beautiful park with themed gardens and lake views.

  • Bukit Timah Nature Reserve Free

    Hike Singapore’s highest point. Lush rainforest.

    💡 Hack: Bring IC for registration at entrance. Free guided walks on weekends (check NParks).

  • Chestnut Nature Park Free

    Large park for hiking and mountain biking.

  • West Coast Park Free

    Family-friendly with playgrounds and cycling.

  • Jurong East Park Connector Free

    Hidden green corridor linking parks and HDB estates.

  • Tiong Bahru Park Free

    Charming park with aviary and fitness corners.

  • Hong Lim Park (Speakers' Corner) Free

    Only place for public speaking in Singapore.

🌳 North Singapore

  • Mandai Wildlife Reserve All-Access Pass Paid

    Entry to Zoo, Night Safari, River Safari & Bird Paradise.

  • Sungei Buloh Wetland Reserve Free

    Mangroves, mudflats & migratory birds.

    💡 Hack: Free guided tours on weekends — book via NParks. Bring binoculars!

  • Thomson Nature Park Free

    Abandoned rubber plantation with wildlife.

  • Kranji War Memorial Free

    Poignant tribute to WWII soldiers.

  • Sembawang Hot Spring Park Free

    Cook eggs in the natural hot spring!

    💡 Hack: Bring your own eggs and a small pot. Free and fun!

  • Kranji Countryside Farms Free

    Visit farms for fruit picking, animal feeding, and countryside dining.

  • Lower Peirce Reservoir Free

    Tranquil forest walk with boardwalks and rare birds.

  • Upper Seletar Reservoir Free

    Peaceful park with water views and trails.

  • Yishun Park Free

    Unique honeycomb-shaped design and fitness corners.

  • Mandai Boardwalk Free

    5km scenic trail through rainforest connecting to wildlife parks.

    💡 Hack: Enter from Upper Thomson Road. Great for birdwatching. Connects to TreeTop Walk (separate entry).

📅 5-Day Singapore Sample Planner

Perfect for first-timers, families, and couples.
Maximize fun, minimize commute!

🎯 Day 1: Marina Bay & City Icons
🌅 Morning: MBS SkyPark or Observation Deck (free) + ArtScience Museum exterior
🌆 Afternoon: Cloud Forest & Flower Dome at Gardens by the Bay
🌃 Evening: Supertree Light Show (7:45 & 8:45 PM) + dinner at MBS or Lau Pa Sat
🚇 Tip: Use Bayfront MRT. All spots are connected underground.
🏝️ Day 2: Sentosa Island Adventure
🌅 Morning: Universal Studios (opens 10 AM — arrive early)
🌊 Afternoon: S.E.A. Aquarium or Adventure Cove Waterpark
🌇 Evening: Luge & Skyride at sunset + Wings of Time show (free from Siloso Beach)
🚠 Tip: Ride cable car from Mount Faber to skip queues.
🌳 Day 3: Nature & Wildlife
🌅 Morning: Singapore Zoo (arrive at opening — book breakfast with orangutans)
🐊 Afternoon: River Wonders or Night Safari (if doing evening tour)
🌉 Evening: Walk Mandai Boardwalk & canopy bridge — peaceful and scenic
🐯 Tip: Combine Zoo + River Wonders for savings on Klook.
🛍️ Day 4: Culture & Shopping
🌅 Morning: Chinatown (Buddha Tooth Relic Temple + street art)
🍛 Afternoon: Little India (street food, shopping, Sri Veeramakaliamman Temple)
🎨 Evening: Arab Street & Haji Lane (trendy boutiques, cafes)
🛍️ Late Night: Bugis Street Market (souvenirs, clothes, snacks)
🍜 Tip: Try chicken rice in Chinatown, mutton briyani in Little India.
🌊 Day 5: Beach, Parks & Hidden Gems
🌅 Morning: East Coast Park (bike, BBQ, coconut water)
🏝️ Afternoon: Coney Island (nature trails) or Punggol Waterway Park
🌇 Sunset: Henderson Waves or Telok Blangah Hill Park (jungle views)
🌃 Night: Clarke Quay dinner cruise or free river walk with skyline views
🚲 Tip: Rent Anywheel bike — under $3/hour. Avoid weekends for quieter parks.

🎆 Best Fireworks & Event Viewing Spots (Pro Tips!)

Marina Bay Promenade Free

The #1 spot for NYE fireworks. Arrive by 6 PM to secure front row.

🔥 Crowd Hack: Enter via Bayfront MRT Exit B.
📸 Photo Tip: Use 10x zoom from Helix Bridge.
🍱 Food Hack: Pre-order from Maxwell Food Centre.
🚻 Pro Tip: Use toilets at MBS before 10 PM.
👶 Family Tip: Bring a baby carrier.

Esplanade Waterfront Free

Wider view, less crowded. Great for families.

🌊 Space Hack: Arrive by 7 PM.
🎧 Audio Sync: Tune into Mediacorp Radio 91.3.
🚲 Bike Hack: Ride from Tanjong Pagar.
🥤 Drink Tip: Bring a collapsible cup.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Group Tip: Meet at the “Durian”.

Gardens by the Bay (Supertree Grove) Paid (Event)

Official NYE party with music, drinks, and front-row fireworks.

🎟️ Ticket Hack: Buy early on Klook.
🍾 VIP Tip: Upgrade for lounge access.
👗 Dress Code: Smart casual.
👶 Kids: Under 6 often free.
🚇 Transport: Use Bayfront MRT.

Siloso Beach, Sentosa Free

Beach party vibe with countdown and fireworks.

🏖️ Early Bird: Arrive by 5 PM.
🚠 Cable Car: Skip Sentosa gates.
👙 Swim Ready: Bring swimwear.
🌭 Food: Pack snacks.
👫 Vibe: Young crowd, music, dancing.

East Coast Park Free

Relaxed picnic-style viewing. Less crowded.

🚴 Bike Hack: Rent from Anywheel.
🔥 BBQ: Book pits via NParks.
📍 Spot: Near Marine Cove.
🚗 Parking: Arrive before 6 PM.
🔭 Gear: Bring binoculars.

Pinnacle@Duxton Roof Free

Best panoramic skyline view. Open on major holidays.

📅 Access: Only during CNY, NDP, NYE.
🚪 Entry: Use 2A Cantonment Road.
🎒 Bag Rule: No large bags.
🌬️ Wind: Bring a jacket.
📷 Photo: Long exposure = epic shots.

Fort Canning Park Hilltop Free

Elevated view over Marina Bay. Quiet and scenic.

🌳 Vibe: Romantic, peaceful.
🚇 MRT: Clarke Quay or Dhoby Ghaut.
🍽️ Food: Grab dumplings from Chinatown.
📱 Signal: Good for livestreaming.

Marina Barrage Free

Open grassy area with skyline views. Fly kites or picnic.

🪁 Fun Hack: Bring a kite.
🚻 Facilities: Clean toilets.
🍽️ Food: Order from Marina Bay Food Court.
🚶‍♂️ Access: From Benjamin Sheares side.
🌙 Night Vision: Let eyes adjust.
