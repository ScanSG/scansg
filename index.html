❓ Frequently Asked Questions About Visiting Singapore

Is chewing gum illegal in Singapore?

Chewing gum is restricted, but you can bring small amounts for personal use.

What's the best way to get from Changi Airport to the city?

The MRT is the cheapest and efficient option, taxis are fastest but cost more.

Can I drink tap water in Singapore?

Yes! Singapore's tap water is safe to drink straight from the tap.
